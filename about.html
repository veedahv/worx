What do WORX really do ?

Women of The Roothub Xtraordinary (WORX) is a community of proactive females from different fields of professionalism ,demonstrating the true essence of womanhood and working together to change the female narrative and foster a gender-balanced,value-added society. The WORX are truly extraordinary in their ways. They do not have magical powers like superheroines do, but their creative abilities and collaborative spirits enable them to join forces and create impacts in their different fields of endeavor. Founded in 2016,the idea of this organisation was to convene women who can join forces to support and empower each other.

Females constitute more than half of the world’s population.This translates to having more than half of the world’s population as women. In spite of this fact,women and girls face the huge problem of gender inequality and lack of empowerment which distracts social progress. More than 50 countries are yet to establish gender equality laws and one-third of developing countries still implement gender inequality in primary education systems.The main effect of gender inequality is reflected in the field of education where many girls do not have opportunities to attend school for stereotypical reasons, familial upbringings and social norms. This limitation leads to reduced opportunities for women in the labor market. That is why women empowerment activities can foster economic growth and promote social development. If every member of the society recognises the problems women face and take step to prevent them,then a change is bound to occur.Some of the social issues associated with females include: violence,trafficking,inaccessibility to proper education and public services,sexual harrassment,child marrriages,female genital mutilation and a host of others. The most important tool for empowering women is undertaking actions of integrating more women in the fields of technology and communications. Creating opportunities to give women access to education will raise female power and provide the world with new professionals.

Our Objectives & Goals

To create opportunities for women in entrepreneurship and provide a platform for mentorship/coaching and funding rewards.

To organise events and meet-ups as a way of unifying and sensitizing women on trending issues as well as appreciate and honour outstanding women in different fields

To train women on digital literacy and skill acquisition and encourage young women to participate in movements that will foster women inclusion,emancipation,growth and visibility.

Vision

A community of women from around the world bridging the gender inequality gap in technolgy and using ICT tools to simplify our everyday lives.

Mission

To enlighten women on the need to embrace technology in their everyday live through mentorship, coaching, training, events and showcasing wome making global impacts with the use of technology and ICT
